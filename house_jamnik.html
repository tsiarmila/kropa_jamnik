House for Sale in Jamnik

House with Mountain View

Discover Jamnik, a little piece of heaven on the ground! Welcome to this authentic oasis of Slovenian style located in one of the most fascinating and popular places in the Julian Alps for photographers visiting Slovenia.

Situated behind the Ratitovec Mountain at an altitude of around 850m above sea level, this vintage house offers a triple exposure. Spanning 120 square meters on the ground floor and an additional 120 square meters on the first floor, this house exudes timeless uniqueness, providing a spacious and inimitable environment for living and enjoying life. Originally built in 1850 and renovated in 2020, this estate spans four levels.

As you enter, you are greeted by a spacious entrance that leads to the living area, comprising a large living room with a genuine Slovenian furnace, a kitchen, a bathroom with a shower, a laundry room equipped with a washing machine and dryer, a spacious terrace and a second exit from the house. The sleeping area on the first floor includes a master bedroom, a dressing room, a home office, and a bathroom with a bath, bidet, and closet. The house features independent heating by heating pump that effective and low-cost, own water source, and sewerage. The house has an underfloor heating system. Furthermore, there is an opportunity to renovate the remaining part of the house and create another separate 200 sqm apartment. The estate also includes a two-car garage, with a ready-made space above it for a potential third apartment. The property is surrounded by approximately 34,000 sqm of land. From the windows of the estate, you can revel in a beautiful view of the mountains.

This property offers much more than just a house; it provides an extraordinary living experience, where comfort and beauty harmonize with the majestic mountain landscape. Come and discover this gem and indulge in the joy of calling it your home.

Price: €600,000 (negotiable).
